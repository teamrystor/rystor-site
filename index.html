The Science Behind Rystor

The Biomechanical Reality of Aging: Loss of Strength & Mobility

As we age, we lose muscle mass in our legs. After 50, this accelerates to roughly 1-2% per year. By our 70s and 80s, the cumulative loss can reach 50%. That muscle is what gives you the power to rise from a chair, climb a staircase, or keep pace on a long walk.

This decline leads to a dramatic drop in quality of life. We engineered Rystor to bridge that gap and get you back on your feet.
